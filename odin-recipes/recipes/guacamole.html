Guacamole

Avocado-based dip, spread, or salad, first developed in Mexico. In addition to its use in modern Mexican cuisine, it has become part of international cuisine as a dip, condiment and salad ingredient.

How to make the guacamole:

  1. Wash and dry the avocados
  2. Cut the avocado in half, set aside the seed. Peel it
  3. Water the avocado with the lime juice and smash it a fork 'til it forms a paste
  4. Cut the tomatoes, cilantro and mix it with olive oil, salt and Seasonings
