Moscow Mule

My personal favorite drink, more than caipirinha

A.K.A. vodka buck, is a cocktail made with vodka, spicy ginger beer and lime juice. It is served within a copper mug, which can be decorated with a lemon slice on its edge.

Ingredients

Ginger foam

  • 2 table spoons of emulsifier
  • 50ml of lime juice
  • 125ml of ginger syrup
  • 250ml of water

You may use a chantilly siphon

Moscow Mule

  • A dose of vodka (250ml)
  • Lime juice (tahiti lime), 25ml
  • 35ml of ginger syrup (Le Lirop de Molin, Gingembre)
  • Ice
  • mint fresh leafs

You my use a copper mug and a cocktail shaker

How to make the ginger foam

  1. Within the chantilly siphon, mix 2 tablespoons of emulsifier, 50ml of lemon juice, 125ml of syrup and 250ml of water
  2. Close it and keep it in the fridge until the time of use
  3. Before serving, shake it and let it rest for an hour

How to make the Moscow Mule

  1. In the cocktail shake with ice, put 50ml of vodka, 25ml of lime juice (the tahiti one) and 35ml of ginger syrup
  2. Shake it well
  3. Strain it within the copper mug with ice
  4. At last, put the ginger foam
  5. Decorate it with the lemon zest and the fresh mint leafs
